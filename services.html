FAQ

Common questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Simple websites take 1-2 weeks, web applications 3-6 weeks, and mobile apps 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. I'll provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes! I offer maintenance packages for updates, bug fixes, and feature additions. Monthly retainers are available for clients who need ongoing support.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, M-Pesa, and other mobile payment options. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront, 50% on delivery for most projects.

Will I own the source code?

Absolutely. Upon final payment, you receive full ownership of all source code, databases, and related assets created for your project.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide. Communication is handled via email, video calls, and project management tools. Time zone differences are accommodated.

What if I need changes after delivery?

I include a revision period after delivery for minor adjustments. Larger changes or new features can be handled through a maintenance agreement.
